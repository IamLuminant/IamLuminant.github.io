About Me

Who I Am

I'm a passionate game developer with 2 years of experience creating indie games in both Unreal Engine and Unity. I specialize in game system development but also have experience with particle systems, asset creation, and game design.

When I'm not working, you can find me making music, drawing, writing, or experimenting with game development.

My Journey

I started my career as an indie music producer before discovering my love for game dev. I then went to BSU to study GIMM and learned how to create games and websites. In my spare time, I also learned another game engine, Unreal. I've also worked with other people to bring their projects to life. Throughout this time, I have continued to learn about music production and have grown as a musician and producer.

I believe in continuous learning and staying updated with the latest technologies to deliver the best games and projects.
